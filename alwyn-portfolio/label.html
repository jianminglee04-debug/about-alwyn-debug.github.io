NOT A LABEL

MASTHEAD DESIGN

Banco typeface gives a retro, bold feel. The first “L” forms a woman’s profile (femininity), while the last “L” becomes a fist (strength). Beige softens the design, and gold highlights enhance visual impact.

SDG Goal 5

Gender Equality

Aligned with SDG 5: Gender Equality, the design emphasizes women’s rights, empowerment, and equal opportunities. The deep purple reflects justice, dignity, and the global identity of SDG 5.

Magazine Cover Design

The split-face composition and torn-paper appearance on this cover represent identity that transcends labels and social barriers. The difference between the two faces emphasizes uniqueness and diversity, implying that identity cannot be reduced to a single definition.

The profile and raised fist of the stylized “Label” masthead serve to further emphasize this notion of the coexistence of feminine strength and beauty. Together, the image conveys empowerment, self-definition, and the dismantling of preconceptions, supporting the idea that each person is capable of defining their own identity.
